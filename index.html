OCBC Cycle Singapore

11 - 12 May 2019
Singapore Sports Hub

OCBC is organizing a cycling event for everyone on 11 and 12th of May.
There are competitive and a leisure classifications for the cyclers. The different categories of cyclers also include Adults, Kids & Families and Corporate cyclers.

To elaborate, there are competitive races for both adult and corporate racers.
There are 2 competitive races for corporate cyclers, one at 6:30 am on 11th May and the second one at 5:15 am on 12th May.

For adult cyclers there is one race on 11th May at 10 am.
There is a table provided below with details about the competitive and non-competitive races as well as their fees.
Furthermore there is an early bird discount for individuals who want to register early, however it will be ending very soon.

8 LITTLE STEPS FOR THE ENVIRONMENT

OCBC is also helping the environment in the following 8 ways:

  • 1) Plant 1 tree for every 50 cyclist.

  • 2) No single-use plastics will be consumed for the event-pack collection.

  • 3) Event bibs will be issued in wood-free paper envelopes.

  • 4) Even medals will be wrapped in tracing paper.

  • 5) Organise workshops on recycling and upcycling.

  • 6) Replace trophies for the OCBC Cycle Speedway Championships with reusable steel tumblers from Hydro Flask.

  • 7) Repurpose event banners and used plastic containers into useful items, such as tote bags and furniture.

  • 8) Compost fruit peels into plant fertilisers.
